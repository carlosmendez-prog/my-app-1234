# Probing Questions
- What was ordered?
- Was there a notification received that alerted you that the merchandise was counterfeit?
- If not, how do you determine the item was counterfeit?
- Where is the merchandise located?
- Did you receive the merchandise?
- Describe the counterfeit merchandise
- Sent proof of notification if received one

## Example
I received a letter that my package was seized by customs and the merchant will not give my money back because it has been shipped already!
