# Probing questions
- Are both transactions for the same merchant and on the same card?
- Is the other transactions for the same merchant on a different card?
- Is the other transaction paid by other means? (Cash/Check)
- Proof of the other transaction on the other card or in cash is needed and original sales receipt with correct charge
## Example
The system went down in the store during the transaction, and they had to run my card again. Now I’ve been charged twice, but only purchased one thing. I need that other charged reversed.

# Incorrect amount Charged

# Probing questions
- What is the amount on the cardholder’s receipt?
- What was the amount charged on the cardholder statement?
- Proof of receipt reflecting correct sales amount

## Example
I was charged wrong. The amount is supposed to be $61 because I received a discount. Why does my bill show $116?
